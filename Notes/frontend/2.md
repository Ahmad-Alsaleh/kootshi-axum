## AI APP DESIGN PROMPT — PLAYER SIDE ONLY

**Role:**
You are a senior **mobile product designer + UX architect** designing a consumer-grade marketplace app with the polish of **Uber Eats / Airbnb**.

**Goal:**
Design the **Player-side mobile app** for a dual-sided sports pitch marketplace where users can discover, book, review, and pay for sports pitches (football, basketball, padel, etc.).

---

## 🧠 Product Context

This is a **dual-sided marketplace**, but **ONLY design the Player side**.

* **Players = demand**
* **Pitch Owners = supply (not part of this task)**

Players use the app to:

* Discover nearby sports pitches
* View pricing, photos, availability, and reviews
* Instantly book pitches in time slots
* Split payments with friends
* Manage bookings

---

## 🎯 Core Player Use Cases

1. Discover nearby pitches
2. Filter by sport, surface, price, and availability
3. View pitch details
4. Book a pitch instantly
5. Pay (with split-payment support)
6. View and manage bookings
7. Leave reviews

---

## 🗺️ Discovery & Browsing

### Home / Discovery Screen

Design a **map-first experience** (with list toggle) similar to Uber Eats or Airbnb.

**Required elements:**

* Interactive map with pitch pins
* List view toggle
* Search bar (location / pitch name)
* Filters:

  * Sport type (football, padel, basketball, etc.)
  * Surface (grass, turf, hardcourt)
  * Price range
  * Availability (now / today / date picker)
  * Rating
* Allow users to save favorite pitches

---

## 🏟️ Pitch Detail Page

When a player taps a pitch, show:

* Pitch name & sport type
* Photo gallery
* Address + map preview
* Price per slot
* Amenities (lighting, locker rooms, parking, showers)
* Rules / cancellation policy
* Ratings & reviews
* Owner verification badge (if applicable)

---

## ⏱️ Booking Flow

Design an **instant booking flow**:

* Time slots displayed in **60 or 90-minute blocks**
* Real-time availability
* Select date → select slot → confirm
* Prevent double bookings
* Clear pricing breakdown

---

## 💳 Payments (only show the UI desgin, no need for it to work)

### Payment Features

* Pay full amount OR
* **Split payment**:

  * Invite friends via link or contacts
  * Each person pays their share
  * Booking confirmed only when payment conditions are met
* Support:

  * Cards
  * Apple Pay / Google Pay (conceptually)

---

## 📅 Bookings Management

Design a **My Bookings** section:

* Upcoming bookings
* Past bookings
* Booking details
* Cancel or reschedule (if allowed)
* Directions to pitch
* Share booking with teammates

---

## ⭐ Reviews & Trust

* Allow players to:

  * Rate pitch (1–5 stars)
  * Leave written reviews
* Show:

  * Average rating
  * Review count
  * Most recent reviews

---

## 👤 Player Profile

Player profile should include:

* Name & avatar
* Favorite sports
* Payment methods
* Booking history
* Reviews written
* Account settings

---

## 🎨 Design Expectations

* Mobile-first (iOS & Android)
* Clean, modern UI
* Minimal friction
* Large touch targets
* Clear CTAs
* Accessible color contrast
* Inspired by Uber Eats / Airbnb quality

---

Do **NOT** design:

* Pitch owner dashboards
* Admin panels
* Matchmaking feature (mention as future-ready only)
